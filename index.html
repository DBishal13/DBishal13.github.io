Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant

I am a research assistant at the Center for Water Resiliency and Risk Reduction (CWR3), whose mission is to empower communities to address the challenges of flooding and rising sea levels. CWR3 achieves this by providing technical assistance, data resources, and policy support to develop watershed master plans, improve Community Rating System (CRS) scores, and lower flood insurance premiums. Currently, in Phase 2, we extend our efforts statewide, creating watershed master plans for all Florida communities aim to enhance their CRS standings and reduce flood insurance costs for homeowners. My role as a GIS Analyst is to simulate floods using CASCADE 2001 based on different storm events along with sea level rise of up to 5 feet, mapping numerous GIS layers, writing reports, and finding solutions to reduce the flood risk probability.
